Inquiries.

Technical specifications and operational details regarding Partner subscriptions.

Can I switch plans later? add

Yes. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Data associated with higher-tier features is preserved but rendered inaccessible until reactivation.

What defines an 'Active Roadmap'? add

An Active Roadmap is a targeted preparation sequence for a specific role at a specific company (e.g., 'Senior Product Manager @ Meta'). Basic users must archive their current roadmap to start a new one.

Do you offer refunds? add

We offer a strict 7-day money-back guarantee for the Professional tier if less than 10% of the question bank has been accessed. Enterprise contracts are subject to custom terms negotiated prior to signing.
