The Problem

The Asymmetry of the Interview

The modern hiring process is an opaque, data-poor environment for the candidate. Institutions leverage vast data lakes, behavioral models, and algorithmic screening to assess talent, while candidates rely on scattered advice and uncalibrated intuition.

Partner. was engineered to correct this structural asymmetry. We transform the interview preparation cycle from a subjective trial into a deterministic data problem.
